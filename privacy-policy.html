Privacy & Policy

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register or make a booking, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Location Data: Bahri may collect your location data (with your consent) to provide location-based services like real-time weather updates or directions to nearby facilities.
  • Device and Usage Data: We may collect data about the device you use to access Bahri, including IP address, browser type, and app usage patterns, to enhance your user experience and improve our services.

How we use your information

  • Booking and Transaction Processing: Your personal information is used to confirm bookings, process payments, and communicate booking details.
  • Service Improvement: We analyze usage data to improve app performance, user experience, and service offerings.
  • Marketing Communications: With your permission, we may send you updates, promotional offers, and service-related information. You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time.
