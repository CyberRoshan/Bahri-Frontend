    Embark on an unforgettable journey to Failaka Island, a destination steeped in history and cultural significance. Discover Kuwait's rich heritage while enjoying a blend of educational and recreational activities.

    • Guided Historical Tour: Explore ancient Greek ruins, traditional Kuwaiti houses, and archaeological sites.
